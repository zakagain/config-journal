# IF (2024) | Review

> "There’s no such thing as a forgotten friend. You just have to look a little closer."

---

### 📋 The Vitals
*   **Director:** John Krasinski
*   **Genre:** Fantasy / Family / "Why am I crying at a purple marshmallow?"
*   **My Rating:** 8/10
*   **Vibe Check:** 100% wholesome, 20% existential dread for adults.

---

### 📖 The Premise
A young girl discovers she can see everyone's abandoned imaginary friends (IFs) and decides to help them reconnect with their now-grown-up humans. It’s basically 'Toy Story' but for the stuff that lives in your head.

---

### ⚙️ The Technical Flex (CGI & Integration)
As a 'tech-person' I thought the CGI was pretty good; it felt as IF (haha, get it? no i'll stop 😔) they were occupying real space. 

---

### 🎧 The Atmosphere & Score
I thought that this music was great. It had the perfect amount of mystery with nice tune. Hanz Zimmer never disappoints.

---

### ⚖️ The "Doesn't Match" Verdict
If you're wondering, yes, this is completely random, and no, it's not going to change

---

### 🏁 Final Verdict
Not a masterpiece, but a good watch. I think you, yes *you* would enjoy it. It's streaming on Netflix, or if that's not your thing, it's rent or buy on Prime Video. Sailing the high seas is also an option. Just saying.
