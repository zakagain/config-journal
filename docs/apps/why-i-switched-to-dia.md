# Why I switched to Dia

> "I went to download [Arc](https://arc.net) and it said try Dia, so I said why not."



### 📋 The Vitals
- **Category:** Browser
- **Platform:** Currently macOS only
- **Pricing:** Freemium with subscription
- **My Rating:** 7/10


### ✅ What It Does Well
It's an AI browser that lets it be a browser without the AI if you want. Also, the AI that's there is pretty decent (better than the braindead Google 'AI Overviews' which are good sometimes, and terrible the others. If you watched Bringus Studios' PS5 Linux video, you'd know.)


### ❌ What It Doesn't
Be a bad browser. 
It's everyone's favourite Chromium base, all support for your beloved uBlock Origin. 
It does what you expect it to do, consistently. 
Never crashed on me once. 


### 🔁 What It Replaced
Replaced [Arc](https://arc.net) for me. Sadly Arc is in depreciation status, but I'm not mad. 


### 🏁 Final Verdict
Can't see myself switching anytime soon!
